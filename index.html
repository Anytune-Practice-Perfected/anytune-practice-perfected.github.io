FAQ

Does it support my music files?

Yes, most formats from your library. DRM-protected files are not supported.

Works with macOS Tahoe?

Fully compatible with macOS 26 (Tahoe), including fixed MIDI support. Virtual MIDI disabled for stability.

Do I need extra hardware?

Optional: iRig HD, Apogee JAM, or similar for LiveMix. Built-in audio works perfectly.

Is setup complicated?

No—import, visualize, adjust. Intuitive for all skill levels.
